## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Original Price">
    Type in the original price of the item you're interested in purchasing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Discount Type">
    Select whether your discount is a percentage (%) or a fixed amount ($).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Discount Value">
    Input the discount amount or percentage you're receiving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Results">
    Instantly see your final price and total savings calculated automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Discount Calculator works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Access it anywhere at [discountcalculator.org](https://discountcalculator.org).
</Note>
