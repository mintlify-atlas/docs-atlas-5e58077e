This guide will walk you through your first discount calculation from start to finish.

## Your First Calculation

Follow these steps to calculate your first discount and see how much you'll save.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Calculator">
    Navigate to [discountcalculator.org](https://discountcalculator.org) in your web browser. The calculator loads instantly with no sign-up required.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter the Original Price">
    In the **Original Price** field, type the full price of the item before any discounts. For example, if you're buying a jacket that costs $120, enter `120`.
    
    <Tip>
      You don't need to include the dollar sign - just enter the number.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Discount Type">
    Choose the type of discount you're receiving:
    
    - **Percentage (%)** - For discounts like "25% off" or "50% off"
    - **Fixed Amount ($)** - For discounts like "$10 off" or "$25 off"
    
    Click the appropriate button or toggle to select your discount type.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter the Discount Value">
    In the **Discount** field, enter the discount amount:
    
    - For percentage discounts, enter just the number (e.g., `25` for 25% off)
    - For fixed amount discounts, enter the dollar amount (e.g., `10` for $10 off)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Your Results">
    The calculator instantly displays three key pieces of information:
    
    - **Original Price** - The starting price you entered
    - **You Save** - The total amount you're saving with this discount
    - **Final Price** - The amount you'll actually pay after the discount
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Calculation

Let's walk through a real example to see the calculator in action.

<CodeGroup>
```text Percentage Discount Example
Original Price: $120
Discount Type: Percentage
Discount Amount: 25%

Results:
- Original Price: $120.00
- You Save: $30.00
- Final Price: $90.00
```

```text Fixed Amount Example
Original Price: $120
Discount Type: Fixed Amount
Discount Amount: $15

Results:
- Original Price: $120.00
- You Save: $15.00
- Final Price: $105.00
```
</CodeGroup>

<Note>
  The calculator updates your results in real-time as you type. You'll see the final price and savings amount change instantly with each keystroke.
</Note>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I have multiple discounts?" icon="layers">
    If you're applying multiple discounts (like a sale discount plus a coupon), calculate them one at a time. Use the final price from the first discount as the original price for the second calculation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I calculate reverse discounts?" icon="rotate-left">
    Yes! If you know the final price and want to find the original price, you can work backwards. Enter the final price, then adjust the discount until the calculation matches.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Does it account for tax?" icon="receipt">
    The calculator shows pre-tax prices. Sales tax is typically applied after discounts at checkout, so add tax to your final price separately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is my data saved?" icon="floppy-disk">
    No data is stored or transmitted. All calculations happen locally in your browser for complete privacy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
